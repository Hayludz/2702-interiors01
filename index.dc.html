The Visionary

Meet Akeju Omotola

Founder & Creative Director · 2702 Interiors

Akeju Omotola is the heart and the eye behind 2702 Interiors. What began as an instinct for beauty became a discipline — a way of turning empty rooms into spaces that hold feeling, memory, and meaning.

She built 2702 Interiors on a simple promise: listen deeply, then deliver beyond the brief. From Tropical Boho restaurants to immersive VIP lounges and monumental entrances, every project carries her signature — intentional, emotional, and precise to the last detail.

For Akeju, design is never about decoration for its own sake. It is about crafting experiences that move people the moment they walk in.
